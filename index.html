College of Veterinary & Animal Science (Haryana)

The Sanskaram College of Veterinary and Animal Sciences (COVAS) is committed to providing the best education and facilities to its students. Our infrastructure includes modern audio-visual laboratories, air-conditioned residential Accommodation : for faculty, and separate hostels for boys and girls with a variety of food options.

About Sanskaram College

The Department of Veterinary Clinical Complex provides remedies for all animals, including wild animals, and our clinical teaching and veterinary extension activities help students develop practical skills. We believe in a multidisciplinary approach to education, providing exposure to a variety of subjects and practical training.


Sanskaram COVAS is proud to house the Sanskaram IVF Center, Genetics Lab & Semen Center, established in 2022. It is India's second lab of its kind, offering state-of-the-art facilities and advanced assisted breeding techniques for dairy genetic improvement. The lab's main objective is to find ways for improving quality semen, sexed semen, and IVF to increase milk yield in the next generation. The lab has signed important cooperation agreements with countries like Bangladesh and Brazil, while delegations from Russia have also visited and shown interest in the lab's possibilities.
